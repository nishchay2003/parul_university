Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, undergraduate programs require 12th grade completion with minimum 60% marks, while postgraduate programs require a bachelor's degree with 55% marks. Specific entrance exams may be required for certain courses.

How can I apply for scholarships?

We offer various merit-based and need-based scholarships. Applications can be submitted online through our student portal. Merit scholarships are awarded based on academic performance, while need-based scholarships require income documentation.

What facilities are available on campus?

Our campus features modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, library, sports complex, hostels, cafeteria, medical center, and recreational facilities. We also provide Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the campus.

How is the placement support?

We have a dedicated placement cell that works closely with industry partners. Our placement rate is over 90% with students placed in top companies. We also provide career counseling, interview preparation, and skill development programs.
