Eligibility Criteria

  • Undergraduate: 12th pass with minimum 60% marks
  • Postgraduate: Bachelor's degree with minimum 55% marks
  • Engineering: JEE Main/GUJCET qualified
  • Medical: NEET qualified
  • Management: CAT/MAT/CMAT qualified

Required Documents

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Entrance Exam Scorecard
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
